About us

#1 Trusted Frozen fish Source, Fresh & Sustainable

Welcome to SFI (PT SUNI FATHI INDONESIA), your premier international fish trading company basedinIndonesia. We take immense pride in sourcing, processing, and distributing the finest Sea frozen fish and seafood products to customers worldwide. With our extensive experience and unwavering commitment to quality, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the global sea frozen fish and seafood products industry.

At SFI, we understand the importance of delivering exceptional Frozen fish (sea and land) and seafoodproducts that meet the highest standards. We work closely with local fishermen, fish farms, and processing facilities to ensure that our Frozen fish (sea and land) and seafood products is sourcedsustainably and processed with the utmost care. Our rigorous quality control measures guarantee that every product that leaves our facilities is of the finest quality, freshness, and taste.

Our product range is diverse, offering a wide selection of fish and Frozen fish to cater to the uniquepreferences of our customers. From popular species like Tuna, yellowfin tuna, Red snapper, barakuda,groupers, kingfish , catfish , black pomfret, shrimp and squid etc, we strive to provide a comprehensive range that satisfies the demands of restaurants, sea frozen fish and seafood products distributors, and retailers worldwide.

We take great pride in our commitment to sustainability. As stewards of the ocean, we recognize theimportance of responsible fishing practices to protect marine ecosystems for future generations. By adhering to sustainable fishing methods, supporting local communities, and engaging in initiatives that promote conservation, we ensure that our operations have a minimal environmental impact.

At SFI, we value our relationships with our customers and prioritize their satisfaction above all else. Weunderstand the importance of reliability, efficiency, and timely delivery. With our robust logistics networkand partnerships with reputable shipping and freight companies, we ensure that your orders are handledwith the utmost care and reach your destination on schedule.

Our dedicated team of professionals is always ready to assist you. We are committed to providing personalized service, addressing your inquiries, and assisting you in finding the perfect sea frozen fish and seafood products solutions for your specific needs. Our expertise, coupled with our passion for excellence, makes us your ideal partner in the international sea frozen fish andseafood products trade.

Thank you for visiting our website and considering SFI as your trusted sea frozen fish andseafood products supplier. We invite you to explore our wide range of products, learn more about our sustainable practices, and get in touch with us for any inquiries or business opportunities. We look forward to serving you with the finest sea frozen fish and seafood products fromIndonesia.
