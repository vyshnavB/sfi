About Us

Providing delicious food products since 2018

Our Story

Eurofine Import Export GmbH is an export and import venture rooted in Germany. Formed in the year 2018 with a vision to fill the hunger needs of those living in the western and central European countries who are thirst to taste the Indian ethnicity. We are committed in bringing Europe the best available products along with the premium standards of Quality at optimum price. Focusing on our vision, to build lasting business partnerships with our clients and customers by offering the best value proposition for the trade.

With involvement in procurement, manufacturing, supplying and exporting of various agricultural and industry-based products, we offer precisely made products by the experienced professionals, who possess an immense knowledge and experience in their respective fields.

We are still at our starting phase, getting to know our customers in a better way, improving on a regular basis as we have to move a long way ahead. We continue to grow our supplier partnerships, logistics networks and global clients. Our proficiency lies in serving our clients with enhanced export and import solutions and we hope to continue with this goal and become a recognized food export and import company with the best services that are the best.

Dreijey

Dreijey is the fruit of that dream of K J Joseph to spread the love and taste of his land across the world with an aim to focus mainly on the consumers need and wellbeing. In the year 2018 we decided to move a step ahead to pursue the dream of reaching out to the people’s heart by feeding them with the love. We started as a small unit and the hardworking helping hands push our venture up in a small span of time to reach up to a mark, we had longed-for. Now we stand as one of the major producers and exporter of food items.
